OUR MISSION

We are a leading company in Kazakhstan providing engineering services for the construction and design of oil refineries. Our history is closely linked to the growth of independent Kazakhstan. Furthermore, we make a significant contribution to the economic development of the Republic of Kazakhstan and the Atyrau region, where we operate. Our primary goal is to create value for the Republic of Kazakhstan. We are proud to contribute to the country's economic growth and its future.
